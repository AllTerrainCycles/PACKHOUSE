How it works

From your store to their door — without you touching a box

Step 1 · Once

Send us your stock

Book your stock in by pallet or carton. We receive, count, barcode and shelve it — and you can see your inventory from day one.

Step 2 · ~10 mins

Connect Shopify

We plug directly into your store. Orders flow to our warehouse automatically — no exports, no spreadsheets, no manual anything.

Step 3 · Every day

We pick, pack, dispatch

Orders placed before our daily cut-off leave the same day with Royal Mail, DPD, Evri or FedEx — in your packaging, packed with care.

Step 4 · Automatic

Customers get tracking

Tracking numbers sync straight back to Shopify and out to your customers. Stock levels update in real time as orders ship.
