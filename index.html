How it works

From your store to their door — without you touching a box

Step 1 · Once

Send us your stock

Book your stock in by pallet or carton, along with your own boxes and packaging. We receive, count, barcode and shelve it — and you can see your inventory from day one.

Step 2 · ~10 mins

We access your store

You add us as a staff account on your Shopify admin. No exports, no spreadsheets, no middleman software — we work directly from your order list each day.

Step 3 · Every day

We pick, pack, dispatch

Orders are picked, packed and ready to leave within one working day on your Royal Mail, DPD, Evri or FedEx account — in your packaging, packed with care.

Step 4 · Every order

Customers get tracking

We mark each order fulfilled and add tracking directly in your Shopify admin, so customers get notified exactly as they would if you'd packed it yourself.

We work inside your Shopify admin

We fulfil from your Shopify store, because we run one too

  • You add us as a staff account — no separate software, no API to maintain
  • We pick, pack and mark orders fulfilled directly in your admin
  • Inventory levels update in real time — no overselling
  • Returns processed, inspected and restocked
  • Works with your existing apps, bundles and flows
